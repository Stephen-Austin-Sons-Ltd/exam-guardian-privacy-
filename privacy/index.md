# Privacy Policy – Exam Guardian

Exam Guardian is an operational application used to confirm the receipt of examination materials at authorised centres.

## Data collected
The app processes the minimum data required to perform its function, which may include:
- Barcode or reference identifiers associated with examination materials
- Date and time of receipt
- Confirmation of receipt by an authorised user

No personal data relating to candidates is collected or processed by the app.

## Purpose of processing
The data is used solely to:
- Confirm receipt of examination materials
- Provide operational visibility via the Exam Guardian dashboard
- Support audit and issue resolution where required

## Data sharing
Data is not shared with third parties for marketing or commercial purposes.

## Data retention
Operational data is retained only for as long as necessary to support examination processes and audit requirements.
